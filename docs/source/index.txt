####################
Cyphon Documentation
####################

Cyphon is a big data platform that aggregates, standardizes,
and enhances data for easier analysis. With Cyphon you can:

* **Collect** data from a variety of sources, 
  including email, social media, and logs

* **Filter** data as it comes in, so it can be 
  parsed, analyzed, and easily searched

* **Enhance** data with automated analyses

* **Create** alerts for important data as it arrives
